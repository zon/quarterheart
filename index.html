QuarterHeart

About

QuarterHeart is developing an untitled multiplayer space combat game. Right now the project is just getting started, but anyone is welcome to try the alpha.

Controls

Move Mouse Pitch and yaw the camera
Q, E Roll the camera
W, S Move ship forward and backward relative to the camera
A, D Move ship left and right relative to the camera
Space, Left Alt Move ship up and down relative to the camera
R Target enemy under sight
Left Click Fire turrets
X Move ship in reverse (only useful when stuck)
Alt + F4, ⌘ + Q Quit
